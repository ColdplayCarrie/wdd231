Why travel solo?

  • Plan as much or as little as you want!
  • If you don't feel like it, you don't do it!
  • If you feel like it, you definitely do it!

In August 2025, I took my first solo trip. I traveled to the United Kingdom by myself, and it was the trip of a lifetime!

Yes, it will push you out of your comfort zone, but You decide how much or how little out of your comfort zone you go.

This website is where I share highlights of my solo journey to help others take the leap into the solo travel life.
