See London

London was amazing! I was able to figure out trains, buses, and walking with very little problems. I used Google Maps to help me navigate everywhere. It showed me all my traveling options and up-to-date information on all train and bus options. London is mostly cash-less. I used Google Pay for everything except at the laundromat where they only took cash. I chose to pay a little extra for a hotel closer to main London mostly for ease of transport. I didn't want to spend 30 minutes traveling just to get into the city. Many sites are easy to walk to. The National Gallery is free. Food was easy to find. There were McDonalds and other fast food places around, but the easiest thing for me was going into the grocery store, TESCO, and grabbing what I needed. TESCO is everywhere, and there are lots of them. Don't try to cram everything into one trip unless that's exactly what you want to do! That's the beauty of traveling solo... you get to do what you want, when you want!
