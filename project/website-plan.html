Carrie Amdal's Final Project: Website Plan - WDD 231

Site Name:

travelsolo.com

Site Purpose:

Make solo travelling appealing to people - Motivate people to plan a solo trip

Scenarios

  • Why would I want to travel by myself? What are the advantages of travelling solo?
  • Where do I start? How do I decide where to go?
  • What should I keep in mind as I plan my trip?
Color Schema
  • Deep Teal: #008080 to be used in headings and backgrounds
  • Charcoal Gray: #36454F to be used for body text
Typography
  • Google Fonts: Montserrat for Headers and Body
  • Google Fonts: Cedarville Cursive for fun things like quotes
